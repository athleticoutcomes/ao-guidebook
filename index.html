Our programming

Ways to train at AO.

Classes and Small Group Personal Training are the heart of AO. Here's the honest breakdown of each so you can find your fit.

Group Class · 50 min

AO Fit

Our flagship, constantly-varied class. Every day is a little different, a smart mix of strength, conditioning, and movement, scalable for every ability level.

Best if you want: variety, a bit of everything, and an all-levels-welcome vibe.
Group Class

AO Strength

Focused on the main lifts with progressive loading and an emphasis on proper technique. You'll build real, trackable strength over time.

Best if you want: to get stronger, lift with structure, and dial in your form.
Group Class

AO Power

A hybrid, HIIT-style format that blends conditioning and strength. Fast-paced, high-energy, and a serious sweat.

Best if you want: intensity, cardio + strength in one, and to leave it all on the floor.
Small Group Personal Training

SGPT

Personalized coaching in a small group capped at 6. You get a traditional strength program (primary, secondary & accessory work), your weights and reps tracked, and consistent accountability, with all the community of a class.

Best if you want: personal attention + a progressive plan, without going fully 1:1.
Capped at 6Tracked progressAccountability
1:1

Personal Training

Fully customized programming built around your goals, schedule, and body. The most individualized way to train at AO.

Best if you want: a completely tailored plan and dedicated one-on-one coaching.
Physical Therapy · Dr. Mari

Move & feel better

In-house physical therapy with Dr. Mari, manual therapy, dry needling, and movement education to help you train around pain and stay healthy.

Best if you want: to address an injury, nagging pain, or move better.
Specialty

Pre & Postnatal

A 6-week, 12-session program designed to support you safely through pregnancy and postpartum.

Best if you're: expecting or recently postpartum and want expert guidance.
Specialty

30-Day Nutrition Reset

A guided nutrition course to help you build sustainable habits outside the gym, because results are built in the kitchen too.

Best if you want: to pair your training with real nutrition change.
How we program

The 4-week cycle.

Every 4 weeks, the whole gym rolls through four training phases together. It keeps your body adapting, your training balanced, and your progress steady.

Week 1Endurance
Week 2Hypertrophy
Week 3Power
Week 4Max Strength

Rolling through our 4-week cycle together.

🔋 Endurance

Higher reps and steady work to build stamina and a strong engine.

💪 Hypertrophy

Moderate loads and volume to build lean muscle.

⚡ Power

Explosive, athletic movement. Move fast and with intent.

🏋️ Max Strength

Heavier compound lifts at lower reps to build raw strength.

Quick answers

Good questions.

What should I wear and bring?+
Comfortable athletic clothes, training shoes, and a water bottle. Bring a towel if you like. Just show up ready to move, we've got the rest.
How early should I arrive?+
Aim for 5 to 10 minutes early, especially your first visit, so your coach can show you around and get you set up.
I'm brand new and a little nervous. Is that okay?+
100% okay. Every session scales to your level and you'll be coached the whole way. Everyone here started somewhere, and we've got you.
Can I bring a friend?+
Please do! Send them our 2-week trial for just $1, and when they join, you'll both get $100 AO credit. See the Refer a Friend section below.
What's the phone policy?+
AO is a screen-free space. Drop the distractions and enjoy the hour, you've earned it.
What if I need to cancel?+
Classes: cancel at least 90 minutes ahead ($10 late-cancel or no-show fee). SGPT: cancel at least 90 minutes ahead ($20 late-cancel or no-show fee).
Where do I park, and are there amenities?+
Parking and on-site amenities info is coming soon here. In the meantime, message us in the app's CHAT or text 512-222-7179 and we'll fill you in.
